Cheesy Chicken

Ingredients

Batter

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2/3 cup all-purpose flour

Fillings

  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 8 oz. mushrooms sliced
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced green onion
  • 2 cups diced cooked chicken
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese divided

Ingredients

Batter

  • 6 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 ⅓ cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cup milk

Fillings

  • 2 cup sour cream
  • 16 oz. mushrooms sliced
  • ½ cup sliced black olives
  • ½ cup thinly sliced green onion
  • ½ cup finely chopped celery
  • 4 cups diced cooked chicken
  • 2 cup shredded cheddar cheese divided

Ingredients

Batter

  • 9 eggs
  • 1 ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 cup milk

Fillings

  • 3 cup sour cream
  • mushrooms sliced
  • ¾ cup sliced black olives
  • ¾ cup thinly sliced green onion
  • ¾ cup finely chopped celery
  • 6 cups diced cooked chicken
  • 3 cup shredded cheddar cheese divided

Instructions

  1. Make crepe batter by placing eggs, salt, flour, and milk in a blender. Blend until smooth.
  2. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Lightly grease the pan. Pour a little of the crepe batter in the pan and swirl it around to cover the bottom of the pan.
  3. Let cook about 1 minute, until surface loses its shine. Flip carefully. (see video for demonstration). Repeat until all batter has been used.
  4. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Lightly grease a 9x13 pan.
  5. Make the filling by combining the sour cream, mushrooms, olives, green onion, celery, chicken, and half the cheddar cheese in a mixing bowl. Place a heaping spoonful in the center of each crepe. Fold ends and sides over and place in prepared pan.

Ham and Cheese

Ingredients

Batter

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, divided
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter, melted

Fillings

  • 2 oz shredded mozzarella
  • 4 oz thinly shaved country ham

Ingredients

Batter

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 eggs, divided
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter, melted

Fillings

  • 3 oz shredded mozzarella
  • 5 oz thinly shaved country ham

Ingredients

Batter

  • 4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 10 eggs, divided
  • 1 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter, melted

Fillings

  • 10 oz shredded mozzarella
  • 1 lb thinly shaved country ham

Instructions

  1. Whisk together the flour and salt. In another bowl, whisk the milk, water, and 2 eggs to combine.
  2. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, along with the melted butter; beat until smooth.
  3. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle at medium high heat, using approximately 1/4 cup for each crepe.
  4. Tilt the pan with a circular motion so that the batter coats the surface evenly.
  5. Cook the crepe for about 2 minutes, until the bottom is light brown and turn the other side.
  6. While you're cooking the crepes, cook the remaining eggs to your liking. I think sunny side up works best here.
  7. Return a crepe to the pan over low heat, and top with cheese and sliced country ham.
  8. Allow the cheese to melt before folding in the edges to make a square. Pop an egg in the middle of each crepe and you're done!

Smoked Salmon

Ingredients

Batter

  • 1/4 cup All Purpose Flour
  • 1/4 cup Milk
  • 1 Eggs
  • 1/2 tbsp Melted Butter
  • 1 tbsp Fresh chopped Rosemary
  • ¼ teaspoon Salt

Fillings

  • Smoked Salmon
  • Cream Cheese
  • Fresh chopped chives
  • Onion Salt
  • Lemon Wedge

Ingredients

Batter

  • 1/2 cup All Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 1.5 Eggs
  • 1 tbsp Melted Butter
  • 2 tbsp Fresh chopped Rosemary
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt

Fillings

  • Smoked Salmon
  • Cream Cheese
  • Fresh chopped chives
  • Onion Salt
  • Lemon Wedge

Ingredients

Batter

  • 2 cup All Purpose Flour
  • 2 cup Milk
  • 4 Eggs
  • 4 tablespoon Melted Butter
  • 2-3 tbsp Fresh chopped Rosemary
  • ¼ teaspoon Salt

Fillings

  • Smoked Salmon
  • Cream Cheese
  • Fresh chopped chives
  • Onion Salt
  • Lemon Wedge

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ingredients for the crepes and mix well.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium-low heat with some melted butter to coat the bottom.
  3. Swirl the batter around to coat the whole bottom and place back on the heat.
  4. Cook for about a minute then flip and cook other side for about a minute as well until golden.
  5. Once your crepes are made and your ready to fill, start out by spreading a thin layer of cream cheese on the inside.
  6. Sprinkle your fresh chopped chives and onion salt on to taste, and layer the smoked salmon on top down the middle.
  7. Squeeze a fresh lemon wedge over the top and fold it all up. Enjoy this delicious savory crepe.

Philly Cheesesteak

Ingredients

Batter

  • 1 eggs
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/3 oz Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
  • 2 oz mozzarella, shredded

Fillings

  • 1/4 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1/4 onion, chopped
  • Salt and ground pepper
  • One ½ pound rib eye roast, ribs removed, thinly sliced
  • Granulated garlic, for sprinkling

Ingredients

Batter

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 ¼ oz Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
  • 3 oz mozzarella, shredded

Fillings

  • 1/2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • Salt and ground pepper
  • One 3/4 pound rib eye roast, ribs removed, thinly sliced
  • Granulated garlic, for sprinkling

Ingredients

Batter

  • 6 eggs
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 8 oz Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
  • 8 oz mozzarella, shredded

Fillings

  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Salt and ground pepper
  • One 5-pound rib eye roast, ribs removed, thinly sliced
  • Granulated garlic, for sprinkling

Instructions

  1. Add batter ingredients to a blender and blend until it has a thin batter consistency.
  2. Combine the Monterey Jack and mozzarella together in a bowl to make a cheese blend.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of the canola oil to a nonstick frying pan over medium-high heat for about 1 minute.
  4. Pour some batter into the pan, let the batter settle, then add some cheese blend and let melt.
  5. Flip the crepe over and let it cook on the cheese side until melted, then remove the crepe from the pan.
  6. Heat the pan with the remaining canola oil. Add the onions with a pinch of salt and a pinch of ground pepper until sauteed.
  7. Place rib eye on a pan. Add some granulated garlic, salt and pepper and cook until the meat is cooked.
  8. Fold a cooked crepe in half, then add the rib eye and one scoop of onion in center of the crepe. Add the cheese sauce drizzle.
