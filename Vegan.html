Garlic Mushroom

Ingredients

Batter

  • 1/4 cup All-purpose flour
  • ¼ tsp Sugar
  • ¼ tsp Salt
  • ¼ tsp Baking powder
  • ½ cup and 2 tbsp Plant-Based Milk
  • 1/2 tbsp Olive oil

Fillings

  • 1/2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1/2 cloves Garlic
  • 1/2 Shallots
  • 30 grams Mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup Vegan cream
  • 1 sprig Rosemary
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Ingredients

Batter

  • ¼ tsp Baking powder
  • ½ cup and 2 tbsp Plant-Based Milk
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil

Fillings

  • 1 tbsp Olive oil

Ingredients

Batter

  • ½ cup All-purpose flour
  • ¼ tsp Sugar
  • ¼ tsp Salt
  • ¼ tsp Baking powder
  • ½ cup and 2 tbsp Plant-Based Milk
  • 1¼ tbsp Olive oil

Fillings

  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 1 Shallots
  • 550 grams Mushrooms
  • ⅓ cup Vegan cream
  • 1 sprig Rosemary
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, salt and baking powder together. In a separate bowl, whisk ½ cup + 2 tablespoons of plant-based milk and 1¼ tablespoons of oil until combined.
  2. Gradually sift the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients bowl and mix to combine into a smooth batter.
  3. In a large non-stick skillet over medium heat, heat a little bit of oil then pour batter. Cook for a few minutes until the bottom is light brown in color and lifts easily, flip and cook for another few minutes until also light brown in color.
  4. In a large pan over medium heat, heat 2 tablespoons of oil and add 2 cloves of minced garlic and 1 minced shallot. Cook for about 1-2 minutes until translucent and aromatic.
  5. Add mushrooms and sauté while tossing for about 5-6 minutes until soft and browned.

Vegan Hazelnut Strawberry

Ingredients

Batter

  • 1½ cups plant milk oats, almonds, soy, cashew, etc
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 pinch turmeric optional, to add yellow colour

Fillings

  • 1 tablespoon powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vegan nutella or any hazelnut spread
  • 1 handful mixed berries

Ingredients

Batter

  • 3 cups plant milk oats, almonds, soy, cashew, etc
  • 2 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoon oil
  • 2 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 2 pinch salt
  • 2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 pinch turmeric optional, to add yellow colour

Fillings

  • 2 tablespoon powdered sugar
  • 4 tablespoons vegan nutella or any hazelnut spread
  • 2 handful mixed berries

Ingredients

Batter

  • 4.5 cups plant milk oats, almonds, soy, cashew, etc
  • 3 tablespoon sugar
  • 3 tablespoon oil
  • 3 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 3 pinch salt
  • 3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 pinch turmeric optional, to add yellow colour

Fillings

  • 3 tablespoon powdered sugar
  • 6 tablespoons vegan nutella or any hazelnut spread
  • 3 handful mixed berries

Instructions

  1. To a bowl, add plant milk, sugar, oil, vanilla extract, flour, and salt. Mix well with a whisk.
  2. Warm up and lightly grease a nonstick pan. When the pan is hot, add ¾ of a ladle of batter.
  3. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes on the one side, then turn it with a spatula and finish cooking for another minute, or until golden.
  4. Spread a couple of tablespoons of hazelnut spread (vegan nutella) on half of the crepe. Fold in half, then in half again, to form a triangle.
  5. dust with powdered sugar, add berries, mixed nuts, and a drizzle of vegan nutella or maple syrup.

Blueberry Parfait

Ingredients

Batter

  • 1/2 cups oat flour
  • 1/4 cup tapioca flour
  • 1/4 cups unsweetened plant-based milk, plus about 1/4 cup extra to thin the batter between batches if needed
  • 1/4 tbsp pure maple syrup
  • 1/2 tbsp oil, I used olive
  • Pinch of salt
  • Vegan butter, to grease the pan

Fillings

  • Vegan Yogurt
  • blueberries
  • oats

Ingredients

Batter

  • 1 cups oat flour
  • 1/4 cup tapioca flour
  • 1/2 cups unsweetened plant-based milk
  • 1/2 tbsp pure maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp oil, I used olive
  • Pinch of salt
  • Vegan butter, to grease the pan

Fillings

  • Vegan Yogurt
  • blueberries
  • oats

Ingredients

Batter

  • 1 1/2 cups oat flour
  • 1/4 cup tapioca flour
  • 1 1/2 cups unsweetened plant-based milk
  • 1 tbsp pure maple syrup
  • 2 tbsp oil, I used olive
  • Pinch of salt
  • Vegan butter, to grease the pan

Fillings

  • Vegan Yogurt
  • blueberries
  • oats

Instructions

  1. In a medium sized bowl, stir your oat flour, tapioca flour and salt. Add in your 1 1/2 cups of milk, maple syrup and oil. Whisk until a thick batter forms.
  2. Heat a large skillet on medium heat for 2 minutes.
  3. Once its hot, grease it with butter. Scoop out 1/2 cup of batter, pour it into the pan and swirl it around quickly to thin it out.
  4. Cook on a medium/low heat for 2 minutes, then flip and cook for 30-40 seconds.
  5. Once done, top or fill with vegan yogurt and berries and sprinkle some oats. Fold and Enjoy!
