Roasted Veggies

Ingredients

Batter

  • 1/2 cup plain flour (all purpose flour)
  • 1 cups milk (I use low fat)
  • 1 eggs
  • 1/4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • Pinch of salt

Fillings

  • 1/4 medium red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup (160g) onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup (113g) zucchini, cut into 1/4-inch
  • 1/4 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/3 cups (56g) arugula, chopped
  • 1/3cup (20g) shredded Parmesan cheese, divided
  • 2 large egg whites
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 large tomato, chopped

Batter

Ingredients

Batter

  • 5 cups / 112.5 g plain flour (all purpose flour)
  • 5 cups milk (I use low fat)
  • 5.5 eggs
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • Pinch of salt

Fillings

  • 1 medium red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup (160g) onion, chopped
  • 1 cup (113g) zucchini, cut into 1/4-inch
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cups (56g) arugula, chopped
  • 1/4 cup (20g) shredded Parmesan cheese, divided
  • 5 large egg whites
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 large tomato, chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400ºF (200ºC).
  2. On a sheet pan, place the peppers, onion and zucchini, and drizzle with olive oil. Toss to coat. Roast for 25 minutes.
  3. Remove from oven and add the arugula and parmesean to the pan, and toss with tongs.
  4. In a 2-cup measure, whisk the egg whites, egg and Parmesan. Place an 8-inch non-stick skillet over medium heat then spritz with olive oil. Measure 1/4 cup of the egg mixture and pour it into the pan.
  5. Cook, for about a minute. When the eggs are set and there is just a little bit of shiny raw egg on top, carefully slide the spatula under the crepe and flip, cooking a few seconds more, then hold the pan over a plate and flip the crepe out onto the plate.
  6. Place about 1/3 cup of vegetables in each crepe and roll up. Serve warm, garnished with chopped tomato

Almond Flour

Ingredients

Batter

  • ½ large Eggs
  • ½ oz Cream Cheese
  • ⅙ cup Almond Flour
  • ¼ tablespoons Erythritol or 1/4 tsp stevia drops
  • ⅛ teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • ⅛ cup Unsweetened Almond Milk

Fillings

Ingredients

Batter

  • 1 large Eggs
  • 1 oz Cream Cheese
  • 1/3 cup Almond Flour
  • 1/2 tablespoons Erythritol or 1/4 tsp stevia drops
  • 1/4 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • ⅛ cup Unsweetened Almond Milk
  • 1/4 tablespoon Butter

Fillings

  • (Optional)Low calorie syrup
  • Berries

Ingredients

Batter

  • 3 large Eggs
  • 1 cup Almond Flour
  • 1 ½ tablespoons Erythritol or 1/4 ts stevia drops
  • ¾ teaspoon Vanilla Extract
    • cup Unsweetened Almond Milk
  • ¾ tablespoon Butter
  • 3 oz Cream Cheese

Fillings

  • (Optional)Low calorie syrup
  • Berries

Instructions

  1. Add all the ingredients into a blender and blend at high speed until creamy and smooth.
  2. Heat a non-stick 10-inch crepe pan under medium heat. Rub the pan's surface with a piece of absorbent paper covered with a teaspoon of coconut oil.
  3. Cook gently, for about 2-3 minutes or until the edges dry out, peel off the sides and flip it over. Cook for 1 more minute on the other side.
  4. Serve with sugar-free monk fruit syrup, keto jam, or almond butter.
  5. Repeat the steps above to cook the next crepes. Always re-grease the pan between each crepe.
  6. (Optional) Add your favorite berries or low calorie syrup and enjoy!

Coconut Berry

Ingredients

Batter

  • ⅔ Eggs
  • ⅙ tablespoon Coconut Oil melted
  • ⅛ cup Unsweetened Almond Milk or water
  • ⅛ cup Coconut Cream melted
  • ⅛ teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • ⅓ tablespoons Coconut Flour
  • ⅙ tablespoon Almond Flour

Fillings

  • Preferred berries

Ingredients

Batter

  • 1 ⅓ Eggs
  • ⅓ tablespoon Coconut Oil melted
  • ⅛ cup Unsweetened Almond Milk or water
  • ⅛ cup Coconut Cream melted
  • ⅙ teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • ⅔ tablespoons Coconut Flour
  • ⅓ tablespoon Almond Flour

Fillings

  • Preferred berries

Ingredients

Batter

  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 tablespoon Coconut Oil melted
  • ¼ cup Unsweetened Almond Milk or water
  • ¼ cup Coconut Cream melted
  • ½ teaspoon Vanilla Extract optional for savory filling
  • 2 tablespoons Coconut Flour
  • 1 tablespoon Almond Flour

Fillings

  • Preferred berries

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, add all the ingredients in this order: eggs, melted coconut oil, almond milk, coconut cream, vanilla extract, coconut flour, and almond meal. Whisk until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly-oiled mini egg pan or a pancake pan over medium/high heat. I used coconut oil that I rub on the pan with a piece of absorbent paper.
  3. Pour 1/4 cup of the crepe batter onto the pan, then tip and rotate the pan gently to spread the batter as thinly as possible. Brown on one side first, cook 2-3 minutes until the sides are crispy, and unstick easily from the pan. The center should be set and dry before you flip over to prevent the crepe from breaking.
  4. Top with preferred berries and fold. Enjoy!
