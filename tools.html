Explore New Fillings and Create Your Own Recipes

Ice Cream

Take your crepes to the next level by adding a scoop of your favorite ice cream along with some toppings. How about some bananas and strawberries with a scoop of vanilla icecream to make a banana split crepe!

Peanut Butter

Try peanut butter with some apple or banana slices and some oats for a sweet nutricious crepe recipe filled with vitamins and protein.

Fruit Jams

What if you could turn your favorite dessert like peach cobbler into a breakfast treat? Try some peach jam with caramelized peach slices with some cinnamon sugar drizzled on top.
